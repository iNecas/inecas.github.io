Enable Horizontal Vim Movements in Spacemacs Magit

Posted on 2019, Jan 23 One min read

One of the most painful things after going Spacemacs full-time has been the unexpected behavior of Magit buffers in combination with evil bindings. Especially h and l not doing what I would expect.

After sitting in my TODO list for a while, I've finally got to finding a solution for this. Luckily evil-magit is supporting it natively, so it's as simple as adding this to your .spacemacs.el. 

(setq evil-magit-want-horizontal-movement t)

Just note, that you need to do so before loading the evil-magit mode that is providing this capability. Therefore, don't use with-eval-after-load as I was when trying this for the first time.
